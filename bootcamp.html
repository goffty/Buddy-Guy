About Buddy Guy

Buddy Guy is a world renowned artisit who specializes in watercolor and acrylic paintings. Buddy's paintings have been showcased in the Art Institute of Chicago, the Metropolitan Museum of Art, and the Louvre. Buddy graduated from the University of Phoenix in 1998 with a B.A. in Art Apreciation and is currently pursuing his masters. Buddy's favorite song is "The Best of Both Worlds" by Hannah Montanna and his favorite book is Curious George. Currently Buddy lives in the Chicago area with his wife, 3 kids, and his dog, Lucky.
